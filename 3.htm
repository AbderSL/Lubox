How to use Lulubox and how it works

Lulubox does not offer a great difficulty in terms of operation. You just need to know well which is the process to be able to apply patches to video games  :

1.    Step 1  : search the list of available games for the game you wish to modify, hack or patch, as you prefer to call it, and click on its icon.

2.    Step 2  : you will see that there are several options that can change depending on the game. You can activate options to increase the number of pieces or the skins infinitely, to increase the speed of the game or to be invincible, between other examples.

3.    Step 3  : once you have activated the options that interest you and they are available, you must click on the lower button that says Install. The patch will install and when you are done, you will be able to access the game and enjoy the benefits of a hacked game.

The operation is based on the installation of patches or plugins which have been developed by other users and which have been shared with Lulubox. That is, the app does not modify the games by itself, except that it uses MOD created by third parties.

You have to consider that some of the improvements that the user can take advantage of if they patch games with Lulubox are not entirely legal because they involve skipping in-app purchases integrated into the applications that offer developers and that, in in most cases, this is the only way that they have to receive profits for the work they have done at their games.
