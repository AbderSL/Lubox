What is Lulubox and what is it used for

Lulubox is an application which is used to patch and fake games in the same way as other similar tools which have existed in the market for a longer time such as Lucky Patcher, Cheat Engine, GameGuardian or SB Game Hacker. It has its own catalog of patches that the user can download and apply to the games he has installed, compatible and included in the application. With it, the user can obtain a better gaming experience by modifying different aspects of the games such as speed, number of lives available, number of coins, score, benefits in the inventory of game objectsâ€¦ at the same time, the user can generate their own patches and in this way create the so-called MOD APK games that he installed and then distribute them.

Its operation is as follows:

1.    Step 1  : access from Lulubox the game from which you should download a patch.

2.    Step 2  : choose which aspects of the same you want to change, such as the speed of play or the invulnerability of the main character to become invincible.

3.    Step 3  : access the patch installation and wait for it to finish.

You may be wondering why Lulubox does not appear on Google Play and it is related to the ethics of its use. Consider that some of its features are related to skipping in-app purchases, and in many cases it is the only way that developers have to monetize their work to have income at the end of the month. If you choose to rig the game for not paying, what you do is equivalent to hacking the application.
